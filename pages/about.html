About Me

I am passionate about:

  • Data Science: Anticipating and uncovering future trends and behaviors.
  • Machine Learning: Enhancing human intelligence with artificial intelligence.
  • Computer Vision: Understanding the world beyond human visual perception.

Background

My background includes interning at Valeo, publishing a conference paper at IEEE EMBC 24, interning at Lawrence Livermore National Lab, and participating in a summer research program. My experiences have equipped me with the ability to build machine learning models end-to-end, comprehend academic literature, and implement state-of-the-art models.

Goals and Aspirations

In the short term, I aim to refine my skills in data science and machine learning to build a strong foundation for my career. My long-term goal is to mentor aspiring data scientists and researchers, helping them navigate their journeys just as my mentors have guided me. I aspire to positively impact careers and lives in the same way that mine has been transformed.
