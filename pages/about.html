About

Background

My background is in Applied Math, and I began programming in 2022. By 2023, I became fascinated with machine learning, inspired by the Photo Stacks feature in Google Photos. Driven by this interest, I soon delved into medical imaging research. Soon after, I joined Valeo, where I developed computer vision models for real-time performance. These experiences deepened my appreciation for the practical challenges of deploying machine learning models and significantly strengthened my passion for the field.

Goals and Aspirations

In the short term, I aim to develop my technical expertise and practical experience to become an impactful machine learning engineer. Long term, my goal is to evolve into a leader who inspires and mentors professionals across engineering and science, fostering a culture of innovation and driving meaningful impact.
